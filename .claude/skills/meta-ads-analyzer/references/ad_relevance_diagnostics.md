# Ad Relevance Diagnostics

## Overview

Ad Relevance Diagnostics help diagnose underperforming ads by providing three independent rankings. Each ranking compares your ad to ads competing for the same audience.

## The Three Rankings

### 1. Quality Ranking
**What it measures:** Perceived quality of your ad compared to competitors.

- Based on feedback signals (hides, reports) and post-click experience (landing page quality)
- Rankings: Above Average, Average, Below Average (Bottom 35% / 20% / 10%)

**If Below Average:**
- Improve ad creative quality (higher resolution images, professional design)
- Avoid clickbait, misleading claims, engagement bait
- Improve landing page experience (load speed, mobile-friendliness, relevance)

### 2. Engagement Rate Ranking
**What it measures:** Expected engagement rate (likes, comments, shares, clicks) compared to competitors.

- Rankings: Above Average, Average, Below Average (Bottom 35% / 20% / 10%)

**If Below Average:**
- Make creative more compelling and relevant to the audience
- Improve ad copy and call-to-action
- Test different creative formats (video vs image, carousel vs single)
- Refine audience targeting to reach more relevant Accounts Center accounts

### 3. Conversion Rate Ranking
**What it measures:** Expected conversion rate compared to competitors with the same optimization goal.

- Rankings: Above Average, Average, Below Average (Bottom 35% / 20% / 10%)
- Only available for ads optimizing for conversions

**If Below Average:**
- Improve post-click experience (landing page relevance, form simplicity)
- Ensure ad promise matches landing page content
- Optimize for a higher-funnel event if conversion volume is too low
- Review audience targeting — are you reaching the right Accounts Center accounts?

## Diagnostic Combinations

| Quality | Engagement | Conversion | Likely Issue |
|---------|------------|------------|-------------|
| Low | Low | Low | Complete creative overhaul needed |
| Low | High | High | Ad quality/landing page issue |
| High | Low | High | Creative needs to be more engaging |
| High | High | Low | Post-click experience issue |
| High | Low | Low | Targeting or creative mismatch |
| Low | High | Low | Clickbait — engaging but not converting |

## Important Notes

- Rankings are **relative to competitors** — they can change as competition changes
- Rankings are **not used in the auction** — they are diagnostic tools only
- Low rankings don't always mean the ad is bad — it may just face strong competition
- Use rankings alongside other metrics (CPA, ROAS) for a complete picture
